Hockey Analytics System

A structured analytics framework designed to support coaching decisions, performance evaluation, and roster planning.

Overview

This system establishes a performance measurement model combining KPI frameworks, SQL-based ETL processes, and exploratory analytical methods. The goal is to provide coaches with decision-ready insights that highlight player contributions, team trends, and tactical opportunities.

Core Components

  • KPI Framework: Offensive, defensive, transition, and possession-based metrics aligned to coaching priorities.
  • SQL Pipeline: Structured ETL workflow processing InStat reports and preparing data for reporting and model use.
  • Player Micro-Metrics: Faceoff efficiency, puck-battle results, pass networks, and event-level contributions.
  • Team Success Index: Integrated measure combining expected contribution, shift value, and positional impact.

Outcomes

The system supports pre-game preparation, post-game reviews, player development tracking, and data-driven strategic adjustments.
