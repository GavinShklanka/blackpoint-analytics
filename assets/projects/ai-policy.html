AI Policy & Governance

Governance-aligned analytical frameworks supporting responsible, controlled adoption of AI in academic environments.

Overview

This project focuses on developing policy-aligned analytical structures that help institutions understand, monitor, and manage the risks associated with academic AI use. The work outlines exposure categories, risk levels, and control mechanisms that guide consistent decision-making and accountability.

Governance Components

  • Exposure Modeling: Identifying where AI tools introduce integrity, quality, or process risks.
  • Risk Segmentation: Categorizing academic tasks based on susceptibility and required safeguards.
  • Control Frameworks: Recommended processes for monitoring, guidance, and alignment across departments.
  • Decision Support: Analytical reference structures for faculty, administrators, and policy leaders.
